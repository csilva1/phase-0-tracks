-Locate elevator in building
-Once elevator is located:
	-face the front of the elevator and look for two buttons on either the sides of the elevator; One button has an arrow pointing up and the other has an arrow pointing down.
	-once buttons are located, press the button that has an arrow pointing up
	-Elevator doors should open
-if elevator doors do open
	-take steps inside the elevator opening until you reach a solid object
	-then turn, facing the elevator doors, stay put
-else elevator doors do not open
	-then wait till doors open
-Once inside the elevator:
	-check on the side of the doors for buttons with numbers
	-press appropriate button that reflects the floor number of your destination.
-Doors will close
-end





-To make a peanut butter jelly sandwich 
-Get 2 pieces of bread and place on counter

	-Then place one hand on peanut butter jar and grip jar, place other hand on the lid of the peanut butter jar and grip 
	-Twist hand that is gripping lid of peanut butter jar, twist counter clockwise off
	-place lid on counter
	-Repeat with strawberry jelly jar
-putting spread on slices of bread
	-grab butter knife with one hand and insert it in the peanut butter that is grasped with other hand, through the opening of the jar
	-scrape some of the peanut butter onto knife
	-take out knife, and with hand that is gripping the peanut butter jar place jar back on counter and gently pick up one slice of bread
	-spread peanut butter on slice of bread
-repeat with strawberry jelly and with other slice of bread
-once both slices of bread is covered with peanut butter and the other with jelly
	-grab the slice with peanut butter and place the side with peanut butter on top of the jelly slice.
