Unidos Para Los Animales

Unidos Para Los Animales is a small animal welfare group based out of La Antigua, Guatemala. We bring in lost, injured, and abandoned animals and nurture them back to physical and mental health. We treat, vaccinate, sterilize, and rehabilitate dogs and cats before placing them in loving homes.
